Paihia, North Island, New Zealand
Wednesday, February 19, 2014

Took airport bus back to the airport to pick up the camper van. All very easy and effortless.van sparkling clean which for those of you who know me realise that is extremely important ! ��   Very spacious van, much larger than we thought it would be and it appears well equipped. Drove from Auckland stopping at a supermarket for provisions, up to the Bay of Islands in the north of the island, taking about 4 hours. We  are now camped by the beach looking out at the birds on the sea and rain. Now that wasn't part of our plan!! 

Cooked dinner in van and washing in washing machine! And no I didn't rush to do it for those who think I love washing! Everything appears very convenient. 

Neither of us feel like doing much and the rain isn't encouraging us to go for a walk along the coast.,Forecast is showing less rain tomorrow. Haven't decided whether to spend one or two nights here yet, think it will depend on the weather.
