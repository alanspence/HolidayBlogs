Melbourne, Victoria, Australia
Saturday, February 15, 2014

Hot and humid day so we decided to head to the coast. Walked over nine miles which surprised even us when Alan worked it out. Hotel very well situated to get into the city across the River Jarra or get to the coast. Not just a coincidence I can assure you! Also at the coast there are fewer flies which we now know about, Melbourne has a real problem with them in humid weather.

Coffee at Port Melbourne at one of the busy cafés. Walked along Coast line admiring the many joggers and cyclists getting fit. Such an outdoor lifestyle. 

Lunch at St Kilda at a wonderful Italian restaurant before walking onto the pier and breakwater to see the wild penguins sheltering amongst the stones. Never expected to see so many.

In Albert Lake Park they were setting up for the  2014 Australian Grand Prix which will take place in a months time. It's a park like Hyde Park which is very close to the city, so glad we didn't time it for when it was on as the crowds will make it too busy. Last night we found it impossible to get a table in any of the restaurants as there were long queues and we hadn't prebooked so those additional tourists would make it even harder to get served.
