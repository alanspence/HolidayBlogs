Hong Kong, Guangxi Zhuang, China
Tuesday, May 12, 2015

Morning free to wander around Guilin before the 16.30 flight to Hong Kong, our last city of this trip! Guilin is a lovely city to walk around. Relaxed atmosphere with people of all ages enjoying themselves. So many different groups exercising, participating in dancing and Tai Chi, bat and ball dances.

This made us laugh today, not what you expect in a city.
