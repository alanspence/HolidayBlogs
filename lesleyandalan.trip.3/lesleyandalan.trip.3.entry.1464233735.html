Grand Canyon Village, Arizona, United States
Thursday, May 26, 2016

Early start for our helicopter ride. Weather was perfect. We were 45 minutes in the air but it went so quickly. There were six of us plus pilot.
Walked several sections along the rim catching the shuttle in between to conserve energy. We thought we were going to get rained on but it passed us by. We have been so lucky this holiday with the weather. Some of the photos are a little hazy but then we can hardly complain.
Talking to a ranger at one of the exhibitions he told us 8 people died here last year. Hikers despite warnings under estimate the canyon. The temperature is 30-40 degrees hotter at the bottom. There is no water so it all has to be carried. Walkers get hot and strip off, not completely! and then die from dehydration as the body sweats and loses too much heat. Rangers walk the trails trying to save people by warning them and giving out salt tablets. Rescues are common.
In the evening we went to a astronomy talk led by a ranger.
