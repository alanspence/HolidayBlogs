Savannah, Georgia, United States
Sunday, May 1, 2016

Relaxed Sunday start before walking around the town. Much of the original layout of the 1700's remains intact. The original plan called for four squares, and eventually expanded to twenty-four squares, surrounded by a network of interconnecting streets arranged on a grid system like many USA cities. It certainly makes it easy to navigate.

We noticed the text for the second day in Washington disappeared - so we've gone back and made up for it with a much longer version!
