Las Vegas, Nevada, United States
Saturday, May 28, 2016

Another hot day. Took bus down to the far end of strip and dipped in and out of the different casino/hotel/shops complexes on the way back. 28,800 steps walked today!! My record. Out again in the evening for the Bellagio fountain show and Mirage volcano erupting.
