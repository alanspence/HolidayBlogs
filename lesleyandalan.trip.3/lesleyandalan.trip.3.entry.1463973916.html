Springdale, Utah, United States
Monday, May 23, 2016

Drove the 17 mile drive following the canyon rim to the south end of Bryce Canyon at Rainbow Point before driving 1.5 hours to Zion National Park area. Our accommodation is on a buffalo ranch but as yet the buffalo have evaded us.

Went a little way into Zion NP and walked to a canyon overlook point for more spectacular views of a completely different type of landscape of red and white cliffs. The vegetation appears lusher already as the elevation is now lower although the overall feeling is still of a barren wilderness.

Where I stayed

Zion Mountain Ranch - East Highway 9, Zion National Park, United States
Bryce Canyon Lodge - Bryce Canyon National Park, Bryce Canyon National Park, United States
