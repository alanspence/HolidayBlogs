Las Vegas, Nevada, United States
Friday, May 27, 2016

Leaving One of the Seven Wonders of the World behind was slightly sad but the contrast with our next destination Las Vegas which is at the other end of the spectrum brought new adventures.

The drive today was through the desert which is definitely much larger than we ever realised as we had never expected such a large expanses of wilderness. Some people have said to drive through the desert quickly but every area from Santa Fe to here has its own unique beauty and certainly doesn't want to be rushed.

Hoover Dam is 726' high and straddles the Colorado River and can be seen from the Memorial Bridge high above the dam. Both are engineering feats.

We had been prepared for what to expect from Las Vegas but nothing really prepares you for the experience. The heat is certainly a shock as we left Grand Canyon at 56 degrees and four hours later at Las Vegas it's 96!
