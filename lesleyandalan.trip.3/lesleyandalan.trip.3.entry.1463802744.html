Moab, Utah, United States
Saturday, May 21, 2016

We were advised by the ranger to get to Arches National Park early so our alarm was set for 6am and we were there by 7.15! On holiday too. We certainly weren't the only ones with the same idea, breakfast was the busiest we have seen.

A steep climb brought us to another world of massive sandstone arches of red and golden sandstone formed by erosion. The 22 mile road takes you nearby to many arches, others required a hike too which really gave us a feel for the area. Well worth doing.
