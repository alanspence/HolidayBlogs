Front Royal, Virginia, United States
Saturday, April 23, 2016

Picked up the car at Union Station and were extremely pleased to get upgraded so we now have a two week old Buick LaCrosse. Amazing how gullible people are with a little polite sweet talk!  

From my Trip Advisor research pre trip I found a wonderful app we hadn't heard of before, Navmii. For those who also haven't heard of it before it's a GPS navigation app which does not use the Internet as it works offline. As we are away for such a long time, sorry to rub it in, hiring a Sat Nav at a daily rate would cost us ££££. It's working brilliantly so far except we have to download each state! Easy for me as Alan took charge of this! It's a worldwide mapping system. 

Drove due west to start the Shenandoah Skyline drive. This National Parks road follows the crest of the Blue Ridge mountains for 105 miles. We had intended to join it further down but a huge 8000 acre fire closed the road. Beautiful vistas reward the drive. As we drove higher it was noticeable that less trees were in leaf as it was too early in the season, but all the better to see the views though. No black bears sighted although someone had apparently seen a mother and two cubs this morning.
