Grand Canyon Village, Arizona, United States
Wednesday, May 25, 2016

Although we have all seen photos of the Grand Canyon you really aren't prepared for the enormity of it. We have been to many National Parks now but this one is very different.
The rugged plateaus, crumbly spires, maroon ridges and all the different shadows all catch your eye. The north and east rim of the GC are 200 miles apart by road. The Colorado River which we last saw at Moab when visiting Arches and Canyonlands NP snakes its way below. The river carved the canyon over the last 6 million years, the exposed rocks are 2 million years old.

Our lodge is set amongst piñon and juniper trees near the canyon rim.

Where I stayed

Yavapai Lodge - 11 Yavapai Road, Grand Canyon National Park, United States
