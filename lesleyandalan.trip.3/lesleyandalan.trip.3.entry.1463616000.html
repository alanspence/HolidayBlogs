Oljato-Monument Valley, Utah, United States
Thursday, May 19, 2016

Monument Valley is part of the Navajo Nation Reservation. The floor of the valley is sand deposited by the meandering rivers that carved the valley, it's vivid red color comes from iron oxide exposed in the weathered siltstone. The darker, blue-gray rocks in the valley get their color from manganese oxide.

Our accommodation has an amazing view from the porch. We did a four hour tour which goes off road to areas only open to tours.

Where I stayed

Goulding's Lodge & Campground - 1000 Main Street, Monument Valley, United States
